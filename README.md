# Game Title
Before the Lights Went Out

A cinematic, single-player, 2.5D narrative game where the player follows the relationship of a father and his daughter. As the father walks through natural landscapes formed from fragmented memories, he must confront his past while deactivating the systems sustaining these illusions. In doing so, the ominous reality in which he now lives is gradually revealed. Each shutdown advances the story toward an inevitable final choice: cutting the main power. The very power that keeps the father alive.

## Features
- Atmospheric level designs
- Environmental storytelling
- Compelling dialogue
- Original soundtrack and some original sound design

## Keyboard Controls
- A, D - Move (Left, Right)
- E - Interact (Press or Hold)
- Shift - Sprint (Only available in certain gameplay areas)

## Xbox Controls
- Left Stick - Move (Left, Right)
- Right Trigger - Primary Interact (Hold)
- X - Secondary Interact (Press)
- Left Trigger - Sprint (Only available in certain gameplay areas)
